Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Who can donate blood?

You must be 18-65 years old, weigh at least 45 kg, and be in good health. A simple health screening is done before donation.

❓ How often can I donate blood?

Safe donation frequency is once every 3 months. This allows your body to replenish the blood cells naturally.

❓ How long does the donation process take?

The entire process takes about 30-45 minutes, including registration, health screening, and the actual donation (5-10 minutes).

❓ Is donating blood safe?

Yes, completely safe! We use sterile needles and equipment. Your body produces new blood cells to replace the donated blood within weeks.

❓ What is the age limit for blood donation?

Minimum age is 18 years and maximum is 65 years. First-time donors should ideally be between 18-50 years.

❓ What should I do after donating?

Rest for 15-30 minutes, drink plenty of fluids, eat light snacks, and avoid strenuous activities for 24 hours.
